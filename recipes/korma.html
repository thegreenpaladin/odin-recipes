Korma Recipe

Description

Korma is a rich and aromatic South Asian curry made by braising meat in a yogurt, nut, or seed paste.

It features a luxurious, thick gravy layered with caramelized onions and whole spices.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (or Lamb/Beef)
  • Oil/Ghee
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Whole Spices
  • Ground Spices

Steps

  1. Fry Onions
  2. Sear Chicken
  3. Spice and Simmer
  4. Thicken the Gravy
  5. Garnish
