Biryani Recipe

Description

A traditional South Asian rice dish that combines long-grain Basmati rice, flavorful curry, and slow-cooked marinated meat.

While its exact origins are debated, the dish traces back to Persian roots and was heavily refined in the royal kitchens of the Mughal Empire.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Yogurt
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Ginger-garlic Paste
  • Ghee/Cooking Oil
  • Spices
  • Herbs
  • Citrus

Steps

  1. Marinate the Meat
  2. Make the Curry Base
  3. Parboil the Rice
  4. Layer and Steam (Dum)
