Nihari Recipe

Description

Nihari is a slow-cooked, rich and spicy meat stew packed with marrow and shank.

It is flavoured with long pepper (pippali), a relative of black pepper and is often served with naan, roti or rice.

Ingredients

  • Beef shank or mutton
  • Beef or mutton bones
  • 1 cup ghee/oil
  • 1 large onion
  • Ginger and Garlic paste
  • 10 cups water

Steps

  1. Make the Masala
  2. Brown the Aromatics
  3. Sear the Meat
  4. Slow Cook
  5. Thicken with Slurry
  6. Garnish and Serve
